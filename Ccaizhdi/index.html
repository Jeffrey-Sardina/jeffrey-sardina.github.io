If you are curious, here is a sample of the language:
  • Meaning: "Drown demons, poison snakes, and turn Deons into Suns!"
  • Romanized: "Roppaestosh shae, zhoxaesh zhox, zai shosh ri aefshosh neft!"

ropaestow wae,
yoxaew uox,
zai wow ri aefwow neft!



Note: Ccaizhdi is written top-to-bottom, left-to-right.

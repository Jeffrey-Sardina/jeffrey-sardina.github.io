subset (ainmfhocal): fo-thacar
sainmhíniú (ga): Tacar a dhéantar as baill ó tacar (nó tacair) eile, nach bhfuil ann ach baill ó na tacair sin.
sainmhíniú (en): S set whose elements come from another set (or sets), and which only contains those elements.

tagairtí:
  • fo-: De Bhaldraithe (1978), Ó Dónaill et al. (1991), Ó Dónaill (1977)
